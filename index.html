Professional Summary

With over 12 years of hands-on expertise in commercial and industrial electrical installations and substation engineering, I have built a career spanning two dynamic regions — the Gulf (Oman) and India. My work covers the full project lifecycle: feasibility, design coordination, procurement, execution, testing, commissioning, and handover.

I specialise in 33kV/11kV primary substations, HV switchgear (AIS/GIS), transformer erection and commissioning (315kVA–20MVA), overhead line construction, and LV distribution systems. Currently operating as Electrical Project Manager, I lead multi-project portfolios across Oman's grid infrastructure, oil & gas, port, railway, and industrial sectors.

Beyond technical delivery, I excel at client coordination, HSE compliance, contract management, and cross-functional team leadership. Licensed under NEDC and OETC, compliant with OES and IEC standards.
