Professional Summary

With over 12 years of hands-on expertise in commercial and industrial electrical installations and substation engineering, I have built a career spanning two dynamic regions — the Gulf (Oman) and India. My work encompasses the full project lifecycle: from feasibility, design coordination, and procurement to execution, testing, commissioning, and handover.

I specialize in 33kV/11kV primary substations, HV switchgear (AIS/GIS), transformer erection and commissioning (315kVA–20MVA), overhead line construction, and LV distribution systems. I thrive in technically challenging environments and consistently deliver projects on time and within budget.

Beyond the technical, I excel at client coordination, HSE compliance, team leadership, and cross-functional collaboration with civil, MEP, and consultant teams. Licensed under NEDC and OETC, and trained to international electrical and safety standards.
