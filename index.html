Professional Summary

With over 12 years of hands-on expertise in commercial & industrial electrical installations and substation engineering across the Gulf (Oman) and India, I bring full project lifecycle capability — from feasibility and procurement to execution, testing, commissioning, and handover.

I specialise in 33kV/11kV primary substations, HV switchgear (AIS/GIS), transformer commissioning (315kVA–20MVA), OHL construction, and LV systems. As Electrical Project Manager, I currently lead concurrent portfolios across grid, oil & gas, port, railway, and industrial sectors in Oman.

Beyond engineering, I'm a self-taught AI practitioner and digital creator — fluent in Anthropic Claude, ChatGPT, and Google Gemini for productivity and automation. I also produce professional video editing, image design, and web development work — making this portfolio dual-purpose for both career opportunities and client project enquiries.
